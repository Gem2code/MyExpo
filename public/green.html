How it can be green

The whole thing could take place online

The books could be online. This is greener as the books would not need to be on paper (which is wood).
The questions could also be online. This way the people would not need to travel to the place of the contest. Less traveling would result in less pollution.

This website is actually my first try to create this greener version

This way, the description is already online. On another page, participants could choose from a list a books according to their age group. And they could also download the books from there. And on another page they could be asked questions about each book they read.
This would also be good because we wouldn't need multiple copies of the same book for more than one participant to read it.
